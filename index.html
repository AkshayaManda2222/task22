Anand Devarakonda

Description:

Deverakonda was born in Hyderabad, India, to Govardhan Rao and his wife, Madhavi. Govardhan Rao was a television show director, while Madhavi was a soft-skills tutor. His brother Vijay Deverakonda is also an actor in Telugu films. Anand Deverakonda graduated with an engineering degree and was placed at Deloitte.
